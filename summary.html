Summary

Demon Slayer is a series that follows the journey of Tanjiro Kamado, a young boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family is killed by a demon. His sister Nezuko is the only survivor, but she is turned into a demon as well. Tanjiro joins a secret society called the Demon Slayer Corps, who fight against demons that have supernatural powers and prey on humans.
